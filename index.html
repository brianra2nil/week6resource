How to deploy on heroku

  1. Go into terminal and type "heroku create"
  2. Gake a mental note of the name given eg: "guarded-atoll"

  3. Go to your heroku dashboard and click on the app by the name created above
  4. Click on resources
  5. Under "Add-ons" type "jaws" and select "JawsDB MySQL" when it comes up
  6. Make sure to selec the free version and need to put in a credit card number, then click on "provision"
  7. Click the box with the arrow beside where it says "JawsDB MySQL", this takes you to the settings and connection info.
  8. Open workbench and close the instance
  9. Click on the "+" sign next to "MySQL Connections
  10. Give it a name under "connection name"
  11. Copy the value of "Host" from the Heroku settings and connection info in step 7, paste it in to Hostname field on Workbench
  12. Copy the value of "Username" from the Heroku settings and connection info in step 7, paste it in to Username field on Workbench
  13. Copy the value of "Username" from the Heroku settings and connection info in step 7, paste it in to Username field on Workbench
  14. Copy the value of "Password" from the Heroku settings and connection info in step 7, paste it in to Password field on Workbench by clicking on "store in vault..."
  15. Copy the value of "Database" from the Heroku settings and connection info in step 7, paste it in to Default Schema field on Workbench. click for visual
  16. Click on OK, and open the new app created in Workbench. Now need to make a use statement with the database name
