How to deploy on heroku

go into terminal and type "heroku create"

make a mental note of the name given eg: "guarded-atoll"

go to your heroku dashboard and click on the app by the name created above

click on resources

Under "Add-ons" type "jaws" and select "JawsDB MySQL" when it comes up

Make sure to selec the free version and need to put in a credit card number, then click on "provision"

click the box with the arrow beside where it says "JawsDB MySQL", this takes you to the settings and connection info.
